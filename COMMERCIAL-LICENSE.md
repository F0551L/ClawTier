# Commercial Licensing

If you want to offer paid services to third parties based on ClawTier,
you must obtain a commercial license.

Examples of paid services that require a commercial license include:

- Paid VPS/bootstrap setup for customers using this project.
- Paid OpenClaw or related app deployment services based on this project.
- Paid managed hosting or operations that materially depend on this project.

## Internal business use does not require a commercial license

You may use ClawTier inside your own company (including for-profit companies)
for internal operations without purchasing a commercial license, as long as you
are not selling setup/hosting/managed services based on it to third parties.

## Contact

To request commercial terms, open an issue in this repository and include:

- Your company/service name
- Intended use case
- Expected scale
- Billing/contact email
